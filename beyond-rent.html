Beyond Rent

Simple rent visibility for owners, admins, and tenants.

Beyond Rent brings rent information into one place. Tenants see what they owe and what they’ve already paid. Admins and owners see all units and properties together — without chasing screenshots or updating manual spreadsheets.

Built in-house by Limitless Lab for Beyond Limit managed properties. Online payments via Casheer (KWPay) are planned in a future release.

At a glance
  • Tenant dashboard showing monthly rent, due date, and current status.
  • Admin view of all tenants across Clover Tower, Villa 41, and other properties.
  • Owner layer for high-level cashflow and missed payment insight.
  • Manual “mark as paid” workflow today, with automated online payments planned.

Account & data deletion. If you would like your Beyond Rent account and associated data to be deleted, please submit a request through the Beyond Limit contact page and mention “Beyond Rent – data deletion” in your message. Our team will verify your identity (usually using your registered phone number and unit) and then delete or anonymise your tenant profile and rent records that we are not legally required to retain for accounting or regulatory purposes. After your request is completed, you will no longer be able to sign in to the app.
