Property & Rent

Beyond Rent replaces scattered spreadsheets and WhatsApp messages with a single source of truth. Each tenant sees their current due amount and history, while admins and owners get a live picture of who has paid, who is late, and which units are performing.

Highlights:
  • Tenant dashboard with live rent status and payment history.
  • Admin view of all properties, units, and tenants.
  • Owner insights into cash flow trends and missed payments.
  • Support for manual marking as paid now, with payment gateway integration planned.
